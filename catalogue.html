Datalivery : Your data services at hand !

Data services

Data Cleaning

We offer comprehensive data cleaning and preparation services to ensure your datasets are accurate, consistent, and ready for analysis. This includes handling missing values, correcting errors, normalizing formats, and transforming raw data into structured, analysis-ready tables. Our approach guarantees higher quality insights and more reliable business decisions.

Stack : Python (Pandas, Numpy), SQL.

Data Analysis

Our team performs in-depth exploratory data analysis to uncover hidden patterns, correlations, and trends within your data. Using statistical methods and visualisation techniques, we transform raw information into actionable insights. This service helps stakeholders make informed decisions and identify opportunities for growth, optimization, and innovation.

Stack : Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn), Jupyter Notebook (easily accessible).

Data Science

We build predictive models and classification algorithms tailored to your specific needs, whether it’s forecasting sales, predicting customer behavior, or segmenting users. By leveraging machine learning techniques, our models provide accurate predictions and actionable recommendations, helping you stay ahead in a data-driven world.

Stack : Python (scikit-learn), Jupyter Notebook (easily accessible).

Data Visualisation

We design interactive and static visualisations, dashboards, and reports that effectively communicate insights to your team or stakeholders. Our visualisations turn complex datasets into understandable, visually appealing formats, enabling faster decision-making and better comprehension of key metrics.

Stack : Python (Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly ), Jupyter Notebook (easily accessible), Power BI & Tableau.
