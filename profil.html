About Me

Hello! I'm Paul Orieux, welcome to this site created as part of a learning project at my school.
I used to be an archaeologist and a heritage mediator at a museum in Nantes. I hold a Master’s degree in Archaeology, with knowledge in both History and Art History. I also have a University Diploma in Art & Culture Law, mainly focused on intellectual property. I've always been interested in new technologies and IT in general. During my studies in Archaeology, I discovered my first databases and developed an interest in data and its treatment, both physical data (artifacts) and digital data (database entries). From the discovery, cleaning, and maintenance of data, to its future use.
After my time at the museum, I started training in Data Analysis, combined with Data Science and a bit of Data Engineering. It was a revelation for me at that time, I knew I wanted to become a data expert.
So I continued my journey at Epitech while searching for an internship to further develop my skills and learn more about the importance of data in our era and its use for the future. My goal is to merge my past experience as an archaeologist with my new expertise as a data enthusiast.
If you wish to contact me, feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn below. Wishing you a wonderful day, fellow traveler!
